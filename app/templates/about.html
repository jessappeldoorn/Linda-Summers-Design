ABOUT

Linda Summers Design Associates is an award winning full service interior design firm. Our services range from custom home design, remodels and office design to full custom interiors including choosing color, furniture, fabric, and art selection... to implementing final details. We identify areas where savings can be achieved and target areas where spending will bring value. We can collaborate with architect's to design a customized home, select and purchase architectural surfaces through planning and placement of interior layouts.

Our Design Philosophy is simple; we are dedicated to creating unique spaces with style, vision and service. Linda has been creating beautiful homes from her studio in the Pacific Northwest since 1994.

PROFILE

Linda Summers has almost 20 years of award winning interior design experience. Her ability to effectively design space and create beautiful, functional rooms has earned her the respect of both clients and contractors. Linda has designed and collaborated on custom home design, remodels and office design. Linda also draws on her 15 years of fine art experience in the Boston area. She interned with Philip Hugh Smith, a noted Boston Interior Designer and was Gallery Co-ordinator for the South Shore Art Center, Cohasset, MA. Linda Summers received her B.A. from the University of Massachusetts Boston, majoring in Studio Art and Psychology.
